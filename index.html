How to get National Identity Number in Tanzania?

To get National Indentity Card in Tanzania, you must have a birth certificate either original or a copy of it. The National Identification Authority provides a free form where you should fill all required information, attaching all required attachments and sumit them to the Office. The board will pass through your information and after verification you will be given your National Identity Number within two weeks after registration.
